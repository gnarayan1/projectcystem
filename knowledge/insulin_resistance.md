title: Insulin Resistance in PCOS

---

Insulin resistance means the body does not respond as effectively to insulin.

In PCOS, insulin resistance can contribute to hormonal imbalance and metabolic complications.

Nutrition quality, movement, sleep, and consistent medical care are commonly emphasized as part of risk reduction.
