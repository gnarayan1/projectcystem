---
title: PCOS and Fertility
source_url: https://www.nichd.nih.gov/health/topics/pcos
tags: pcos, fertility
---

PCOS may affect ovulation, which can make conception more difficult for some people.

Many individuals with PCOS can still become pregnant with appropriate support and treatment.

Fertility planning should be discussed with a licensed clinician, who can tailor options to individual medical history and goals.
